Computational Chemistry Journal Club

Welcome to the club! We're a group of students interested in discussing computational chemistry papers.

About Us

Our goal is to explore interesting topics beyond the standard curriculum, improve our understanding of current research, and foster discussions in a relaxed environment. We select papers that are generally understandable with undergraduate level math/physics/chem.

How it Works

We typically meet once in a fortnight (tentatively) to discuss a pre-selected paper. One member usually volunteers to lead the discussion, providing a brief overview and highlighting key points or questions. Active participation and questions from everyone are highly encouraged!
